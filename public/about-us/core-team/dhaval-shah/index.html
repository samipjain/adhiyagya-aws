Core Team
Dhaval Shah

Dhaval Shah has been associated as a member since the inception of Adhiyagya. He is a Chartered Accountant by profession based in Mumbai working for a global accounting and a consulting firm. As a team member at Adhiyagya he has the primary responsibility of handling the inbound and outbound electronic communications in addition to assisting the other team members to enhance Adhiyagya’s presence across relevant masses. He has always striven for societal improvement and is a strong believer that education in today’s world is the biggest differentiator between a progressive and a regressive stagnant society. His idea of a bright future for children aligns perfectly with Adhiyagya’s vision and mission emphasizing on ‘Education for All’. His sincere efforts and determination make him an indispensible part of Team Adhiyagya.

“It gives me immense pleasure in supporting Adhiyagya in this smallest way of providing back end support. Somewhere, it gives me content that this act of enabling smooth communication with people associated and willing to be associated with us, will bring goodwill and that way I will make sure that the kids at Adhiyagya will be empowered through education and given an equal opportunity in our society. Glad to be associated with Adhiyagya.”

– Dhaval Shah
