  • Equal and Quality Education for All
    Posted Date October 29, 2018

    Education is not just a word. It means imparting knowledge to create hope and values for dignity of life. Education is a solution to so many problems prominent in the world. It not only polishes our mind but also enhances our personality and attitude.

    The world is divided into two categories of developed and developing nations. The nation's progress depends upon two factors: it's literacy rate and health.

    Modern education system is fully capable to remove the social issues of illiteracy and inequality among people of different race, religion and caste. When we go to stats we find the percentage of students attending schools has increased yet the fact can't be denied that standard and quality of education has declined.

    Why is the standard of education declining in times of technical advancement? The reasons are easy to detect but difficult to accept.

    Poor facilities like building, sanitation, improper training of teachers and lack of awareness for education are factors that influence the negative growth and makes it difficult.Â  Children of rural areas and unprivileged children are attending schools but they fail even to read and do basic maths. The teachers are either not trained or they aren't willing to make efforts. The literary rate of private school can easily be compared with government schools.

    If every child is able to get quality education they can break from the cycle of poverty and create new ways of employment for a better tomorrow. Education also broadens our minds to break stereotypes of inequalities to reach gender equality. It helps to understand the importance of personal hygiene and health.

    Man is a social animal. We called ourselves from wanderers to civilized man when we learnt to construct, realised importance of being together for our safety and happiness. But today, we fail to understand that for a safe, peaceful world we need to understand each other and live in harmony. Education helps to understand the world and society in a better way.

    As an educated person, it is very easy to talk, comment and point fingers towards the inefficient system. But it is we, who are actually lacking. A country cannot progress without collective efforts of each individual.

    People like Babar Ali, NGO'S like Adhiyagya and even UN under its SDG(sustainable development goals) have realised importance of education for all and striving hard to provide quality education based on creating value to life.

    So, rather than blaming start taking initiative. If you want to make a positive difference in the society work towards a future that is more humane.

    "Teach others to best of your ability, whether it is a sentence or a phrase". If we actually follow this quoted phrase the world will surely be a better place.

    Let us all along with government take personal initiative to provide quality education to the children including impoverished families. Children need to be encouraged by providing scholarships and other tokens of appreciation. Teachers training workshops should be conducted. Facilities involving school building and access to water and electricity in schools should be made. The role of teachers should be frequently monitored and the curriculum should be based on inclusive education for development and growth of every child.

    LET'S HAVE EQUAL AND QUALITY EDUCATION FOR ALL

    – Nupur Aggarwal
