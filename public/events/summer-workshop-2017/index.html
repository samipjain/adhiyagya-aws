Summer Workshops

Sarita Vihar
A six-days workshop was conducted for the students of Adhiyagya during their summer vacations. The main focus was on English listening and writing skills. Interesting techniques such as story recitation through audio visuals and role play were adopted to enhance their listening skills. Another session for basic Mathematics concepts such as Percentage, Simple Interest and Profit/Loss was also conducted. An overall assessment was conducted on the last day and the students showed remarkable improvement.
Sector 45, Faridabad
Kids learnt the art of making pens stands with the help of our volunteers at our Faridabad centre. The children showed remarkable creativity and made very beautiful and colourful pen stands.
International Yoga Day Celebrations
This year once again our children participated in the International Yoga Day celebrations at Sarita Vihar. They performed all the Yog Kriyas and Asanas and also gave a special performance by singing Adhiyagya's theme song – Desh Hume Deta hai Sab Kuch.
