Diwali Celebrations – October 2016

Rajasthani Camp, Sarita Vihar & Sector 45, Faridabad
Diwali was celebrated this year at both our centres with complete traditions and a lot of fun. As per the Adhiyagya tradition, the children were given diyas which they decorated beautifully using paints, sparkles etc. The centres were first cleaned and then decorated beautifully with lights, flowers, diyas, candles and other decoration material by the children & the volunteers. Beautiful rangolis were also made by some of our volunteers and children. Thereafter, the Laxmi & Ganesh puja was conducted and some children came forward and explained the significance of Diwali to everyone present there. The Prasad was distributed amongst everyone after that and the children wished all the volunteers and guests present there and took their blessings.
