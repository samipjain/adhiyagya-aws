Sports Day – One Year Celebration – 10th April 2016

To commemorate Adhiyagya’s one-year journey, a Sports Day was organized for the children on Sunday, 10th April 2016 at Sarita Vihar. 110 children participated in many competitions, coordinated by the efforts of 30 volunteers and witnessed by more than 150 guests. The atmosphere was absolutely charged on the beautiful Sunday morning with the children dressed in their whites with blue & orange Adhiyagya caps, soaring with excitement. The racing track markings on the play field gave a clear picture to the guests of what the morning had in store.
The event began with a group song – ‘Desh Humein Deta Hai Sab Kuch’ sung by all the children and volunteers. It was followed by march-past by the boys proudly waving the Indian flag. Thereafter children performed Yoga demonstrating various Asanas and Pranayam. After that the boys and girls in separate groups made some amazing pyramid formations that left the audience in awe. It was followed by an energetic aerobics drill with colorful pom poms to the song ‘Jai Ho’.
Then it was time for the main event, the racing competitions. All the children were divided into groups according to their age. Baton relay and various hurdle races such as spoon and lemon race, one-legged race, three-legged race etc. were held and the children participated with full vigor. The winners were awarded prizes and participation certificates were given to all the children. Thereafter began the ‘Tug of War’ competition. While the children gave each other tough competition and had a lot of fun, many people from the audience also came forward and participated in the Tug of War challenge. The program ended with the felicitation of the volunteers associated with Adhiyagya, followed by the National Anthem.
The hard work put in by the children was evident throughout the program. A whole lot of competitions and games, wins and losses, and the underlying spirit of teamwork made this a wholesome event. The children were elated and they went home with smiling faces. We are thankful to Lions Club Delhi Lotus for sponsoring the event and to all the guests for being a part of our celebration.
