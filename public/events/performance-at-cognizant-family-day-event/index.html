Performance at Cognizant's Family Day Event

The children of Adhiyagya got the opportunity to perform at Cognizant’s Family Day Event that was celebrated on 3rd December 2016 at the Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium. The performance of our children began with Adhiyagya’s song – ‘Desh Hume Deta Hai Sab Kuch’. Thereafter the children performed a nukkad natak on cleanliness and gave out a strong message as to how individually we can all be a part of the cleanliness drive. Their performance ended with a very special act where the children sang a fusion of Hum hone Kamyaab & We Shall overcome and simultaneously displayed placards of how they aspired to someday work at Cognizant. The audience applauded the children. Our children thereafter had a wonderful time as they danced to the DJ, ate yummy food and got loads of pictures clicked at the photo booth. The children were ecstatic with their first outdoor performance and stated it to be the ‘Best Day Ever’.
Thank you Cognizant for having us over and a special thank you to Vivek, Kirti & Mayank for managing everything.
