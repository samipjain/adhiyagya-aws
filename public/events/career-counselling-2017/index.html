Career Counselling

Sarita Vihar - 28th May 2017
A career counseling session was organised at our Sarita Vihar Centre to understand the uniqueness of each child and to guide him/her accordingly. Our counselors, Akshta and Pooja used various techniques to understand the interests and goals of every child. Every child was motivated and suggested a career path keep their circumstances in mind.
