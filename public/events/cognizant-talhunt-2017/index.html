Cognizant's TalHunt 2017

Adhiyagya participated in TalHunt 2017 on 10th September 2017, an annual talent hunt competition where various NGOs participate in different competitions. Over 43 children from Adhiyagya competed with children from 8 other schools in 19 different events such as singing, dancing, extempore, best out of waste, drawing, quiz etc. Our children won 11 prizes in all out of which 5 of them were the First prize. Adhiyagya also bagged the 1st Runners-Up trophy at the event. The children had worked really hard for the event and were ecstatic after the win.
