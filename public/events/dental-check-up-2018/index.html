Dental Check Up – December 2018

A camp for dental check up was organised for all the children at our Faridabad centre. The volunteers from HASNAA (Healthy affordable solution nationality for Aam Aadmi) conducted this camp. Their team of doctors & volunteers taught the children ticks and tricks of oral hygiene in an interactive and fun manner. Thereafter dental check up was conducted for every child. It was a very enlightening session.
We are thankful to the team from HASNAA for their time and efforts.
