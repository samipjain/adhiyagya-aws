Cleanliness Campaign

Sector 45, Faridabad â€“ 7th August 2016
Our children and volunteers at the Faridabad center conducted a weeklong cleanliness campaign in Faridabad from 18th to 24th December 2016. The campaign was an endeavor to create awareness among our children and their families who live in unhygienic conditions and also an attempt to create consciousness among the audience at large through our social media handles. Various sensitive topics such as open defecation, waste management, dignity of our rag pickers etc. were touched upon sending out a strong message about the importance of a clean environment. A video was made where our volunteers are seen pledging to the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and encouraging others to work towards a cleaner India. An awareness drive was carried out in the nearby slums from where our children come. They were educated about the problems that they could face due to open defecation and living in a dirty environment. Various government schemes were also shared with them so that they could get toilets made at their homes. A health & hygiene talk was conducted for our children to explain to them the small changes that they must adopt in their daily lives for a healthier lifestyle. A rally was also carried out in Sector 45, Faridabad where the children & volunteers of Adhiyagya marched shouting slogans for a clean & healthy environment. The children were seen holding beautiful & informative posters, strongly conveying the message. The cleanliness week ended on 24th December however it is imperative to understand that this movement is an ongoing one and Adhiyagya is continuously working towards it to bring about the change we wish to see.
