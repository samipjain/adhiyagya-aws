Kunjal Sehgal

Kunjal Sehgal is a Chartered Accountant by profession who was always driven to serve the society. After her schooling from Auckland House School, Shimla and DPS Mathura Road, she went on to pursue her B.Com (Honors) from Hansraj College, Delhi University. Thereafter, she cleared her C.A. exams and was offered a job at a bank in Mumbai.
After living, what could be a corporate dream for most fresh graduates out there, she decided to pursue what was her deepest desire, to serve the society. Having spent many days thinking about how she would venture into the social sector, one day, after two and a half years into the job, she decided to take the plunge. She quit her job and went against societal norms to take a small step towards realizing her dream. After experiencing various aspects of social work she was determined thatEducation was a force that could resolve the inequality and disparity in the society and thus founded Adhiyagya.
Motivated, focused and determined are the three words that best define her and she has always believed that no one can go back in time and change a bad beginning but anyone can start anything anytime and create a successful ending.

“With the belief that Education can bring about a massive change in the society, foundation of Adhiyagya was laid. And as we slowly tread on this path of knowledge with full determination and perseverance, I strongly believe that we shall be able to achieve our objective. Even in this short span, being able to bring a small change in the lives of so many children has been immensely satisfying and this is just the beginning!”
– Kunjal Sehgal
