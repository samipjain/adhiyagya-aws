Core Team
Mr. Gopal Mehandru

Mr. Gopal Mehandru is a Mechanical Engineer from MS University, Baroda. He has a vast experience of over 35 years in the field of Piping Design and construction in the capacity of Director.

He has handled projects related to oil and gas, thermal power stations, chemicals and other industrial applications. Along with that, he has undertaken many turn-key projects that fell into the category of Energy Conservation. He was associated with overseas Engineering Companies offering Services and marketing their software solutions for the engineering requirements. He has also been associated with companies offering Renewable Energy Services like Solar power. Also ventured into Reverse Engineering activities to create workable engineering drawings of old and undocumented Installations.

Apart from this, he has counseled students and families for overseas education along with his wife Mrs. Renu Mehandru. For over 25 years they have been instrumental in helping students take up foreign education and career in North America.

Mr. Mehandru has a strong hold over administration, organizational and counseling skills. His presence as an advisor will bring in profound experience for guiding the children and assisting them to take the appropriate career and vocational decisions. Further, his elaborate career expertise at turn key engineering and renewable energy projects serves as a source of inspiration for the children at Adhiyagya. He is a firm believer of the saying – “Education creates doors of opportunities which can be unlocked by the keys of teaching.”
