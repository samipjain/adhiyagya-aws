Prof. Mohini Anjum

Prof. Mohini Anjum pursued her MA in Sociology and thereafter completed her PhD in Sociology from Delhi School of Economics, Delhi University (DU). She has an enriching experience of over 36 years in the field of teaching and research. She started her career as an Honarary Lecturer at Delhi School of Economics, DU. Then she joined Hindu College, DU as a lecturer. In the year 1976, Prof. Anjum joined Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi as a Reader, thereafter became a Professor and later retired as the Head, Department of Sociology in the year 2004.

She has also authored a book namely ‘Muslim Women in India’ and co-authored ‘Inter-religion Marriages in India’ with Mrs. Arwinder Ansari. A large number of articles written by her have been published in books and journals. Prof. Anjum has done extensive research in the field of Gender Studies, Minority Studies, Women Studies in India, Iran, Canada, Bahrain etc and Indian Diaspora.

Being an educationist she brings with her vast experience of mentoring children to pursue the path of education to succeed in life. She not only provides guidance and motivation but is also instrumental in providing an insight through her extensive professional experience. At Adhiyagya, she is influential in developing road map for creating social and cultural awareness programs for children. Her guidance is always sought to disseminate social messages like women empowerment, gender equality and unity in diversity to children through the curriculum and events at Adhiyagya.
