Independence Day Celebrations – August 2017

Sarita Vihar - 15th August 2017
Independence Day celebrations were conducted this year on 15th August at our Sarita Vihar centre and the theme for this year was #TributeToSoldiers. The children had prepared a wonderful show that highlighted the rich culture our country has and also the current topics that require our attention. The show began with Sloka chanting and was followed by our girls singing the National Song – Vande Mataram. Thereafter the boys performed a dance on ‘Ye Desh hai Veer Jawano ka' and the girls dance to the tunes of ‘Suno gaur Se Duniya Waalon’. The show included two skits; one was based on the theme Unity in Diversity while the other focused on Waste Segregation under the Swachh Bharat Campaign. The show concluded with all the children singing Adhiyagya’s song – Desh Hume Deta Hai Sab Kuch followed by a flash mob on Chak De India.
Sector 45, Faridabad - 13th August 2017
As a part of the Independence Day celebration at our Faridabad centre, a workshop was conducted by the employees of Cognizant on 13th August 2017. Children learnt the art of kite making and made beautiful and colourful kites. A talk was also held for boys and girls separately focusing on various social evils. The children danced and sang with the volunteers from Cognizant and had a wonderful time.
