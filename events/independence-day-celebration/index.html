Independence Day Celebration – 15th August 2015

Our first celebration at Adhiyagya this year was the Independence Day. It was done as an exercise to boost the self-esteem of the kids. This was a big day for the kids as it was the first time our entire strength was going to perform in front of a crowd other than their family and friends.
It would be unjust not to mention how much hard work the kids had put in to make this a success. While practice went on for crazy hours, the zeal of the kids did not fall low at anytime. Anyone who saw them practicing could gauge that they were determined to put up a good show. And YES, a good show it was! The success of this function was a small but an extremely important step towards reassuring these kids that anything can be accomplished with hard work.
On 15th August 2015 the kids put up a wonderful show before the residents of Sarita Vihar at the C Block Community Centre, Sarita Vihar.They danced, sang, introduced the audience to the life of Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam and also performed a skit. Pertinently, the entire program was conducted in English and succeeding in something like this gave these kids their lost self-confidence. While each of the performances taught the kids a moral lesson, the biggest lesson they took home that day was that hard work conquers all. It was very encouraging for the entire team of Adhiyagya to see how inspired the kids were post the function and they had all reassured their faith in themselves.
For the first time we also genuinely believed when the kids sang, “We shall overcome someday” that we certainly shall overcome some day!
