Children’s day – 14th November 2015

Children’s day was celebrated at Adhiyagya on 14th November 2015 with 105 kids. The planning of the entire event commenced just one-week prior, still there were more than 50 kids who participated demonstrating their talent. The day began with chanting of Shlokas followed by beautiful dance performances, rejuvenating songs and amazing skit performances spreading positive messages to society like empowering woman,education and stand against dowry. All the performances were in groups and kids guided each other during practice sessions showcasing their attitude of “team spirit”. Kids were super excited about the day and had enormous fun throughout the event. All the friends and volunteers added charm to the event by exchanging their encouraging thoughts with the kids and actively participating in the event.
