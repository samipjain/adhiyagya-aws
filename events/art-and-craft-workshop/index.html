Art and Craft Workshop

Sarita Vihar, New Delhi - June 2018
A two-days Art and Craft workshop was conducted at our Sarita Vihar Centre by Ms. Nishat and Mr. Sanjeev. On the first day our kids were taught how to make a puppet cum penholder out of waste plastic bottles. The instructors explained the basic procedure to the children. However the kids used their creativity in creating different faces of animals, coloring the bottles, using thread for limbs etc. As a result each one of them had a recycled yet beautiful holder for storing their pencils and pens. The same could also be utilized as a decorative item.
On the second day, they created beautiful flowers with the help of coloured sheets of paper. The instructors gave amazing trick on cutting and folding the paper in a special way to create a blooming flower in no time. A huge thanks to our instructors for conducting this workshop. The children had a wonderful time and learnt a lot.
Faridabad - October 2018
The volunteers conducted an art and craft workshop for the children and taught them how to make different animal faces with a paper-folding twist. The children made beautiful and colourful drawings and had a wonderful session.
