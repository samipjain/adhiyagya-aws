Children's Day Celebration 2016 – Sarita Vihar

Rajasthani Camp, Sarita Vihar – 20th November 2016
On Sunday, 20th November, Cognizant organized an event for the children of Adhiyagya to celebrate Children’s Day at our Sarita Vihar centre. The event included fun games and competition where over 90 children participated. It began with a relay race and an obstacle race followed by playing Dog & the Bone. Thereafter, the kids gorged on the Gol Gappas and savored the wonderful lunch that was arranged for them. Subsequently the children were divided into multiple teams and given some stationery and craft material. They were asked to choose a topic and depict it through a story using all the items made available to them. The children made some wonderful stories focusing on topics such as pollution, education, freedom, respect for woman etc. This acted as a knowledgeable as well as a wonderful team building activity for the children.
We would like to thank the team from Cognizant for such a wonderful initiative; the children had an amazing time.
