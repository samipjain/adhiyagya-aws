Mini Library

“Reading is to the mind what exercise is to the body”
– Joseph Addison

It is often said that a reader lives a thousand lives. With such a vision in our minds, we thought its essential to inculcate a habit of reading among our little stars. Consequently, we have set up a mini library at our center in Rajasthani Camp, Sarita Vihar. The kids are encouraged to sit and read at the library during their idle time and are also allowed to issue books. Our endeavor is to create a conducive environment for learning, close to where the children live. With the establishment of a library, we have successfully created an environment that facilitates studies which is often lacking at the homes of the children being mentored by our organization.

Our library at present has around 110 books comprising of moral stories, fiction, biography etc. At this juncture, we would like to take a moment to mention the contribution of Mrs. Aruna Jain, a resident of Sarita Vihar who donated a whole lot of books initially and helped us set up this library. We are also grateful to our well–wishers who have kept the books coming. The kids are thrilled to have many interesting and value based books for their library.

“Once a child starts enjoying reading, books will be like a computer in

his/her hand where there is no end to learn more and more. The

enjoyment of reading will lead them to know more and seek more books.

The sky will be the limit – The contribution of this small collection of

books is a small beginning.”

– Aruna Jain
