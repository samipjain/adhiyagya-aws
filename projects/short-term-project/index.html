Interactive Sundays

The project ‘Interactive Sundays’ attempts at bringing people from all walks of life to come forward and interact with the children. The aim is to broaden the vision of our children through the life experiences of different people and learn from them. This project includes sessions in the form of various fun and interactive activities, motivational sessions, career counseling and many other things. At Adhiyagya, we believe that children learn the most through their experiences and observations therefore instead of preaching good values and habits through literature it is endeavored that the children themselves interact with new people and incorporate in themselves the good virtues they observe.

As a part of this project, we have also partnered with Igniting Young Minds (a wing of the Delhi University organization – Leaders For Tomorrow) wherein students from Delhi University colleges come forward for 8 weekly sessions and interact with the children. The volunteers take up various activities that help children in improving their English speaking, General Knowledge and also help them prepare for their examinations. The first such project took place in September-October 2015 with 7 volunteers and the second project happened in January-February 2016 with 9 volunteers from IYM.

Also from time to time various distinguished guests come forward and enlighten the children through this platform.
